## FAQ (5 real Q&As)

**Q1: Is this actually free?**
A1: Yes. The audit is completely free with no login required. We show you the results first, then optionally ask for your email to send a copy. We never ask for payment.

**Q2: How accurate are the savings numbers?**
A2: All pricing data is sourced directly from official vendor pricing pages and verified regularly. The audit engine uses hardcoded rules — no AI guesswork in the math. If a vendor changes their pricing, we update the engine.

**Q3: What does Credex do?**
A3: Credex is an AI infrastructure credits company. For teams spending heavily on AI APIs (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google), Credex negotiates bulk credits that reduce your effective per-token cost. The audit surfaces this as an option when it makes financial sense — we don't push it when it doesn't.

**Q4: Will you spam me?**
A4: No. You get one confirmation email with your audit results. If your savings are above $500/month, a Credex team member may reach out once — only if you've submitted your email. No drip campaigns, no newsletters unless you opt in.

**Q5: Can I share my audit results with my team?**
A5: Yes. Every audit gets a unique public URL you can share with your CTO, CFO, or team. The public view strips your email and company name — only tool data and savings numbers are shown. It's designed to be screenshot-worthy.
